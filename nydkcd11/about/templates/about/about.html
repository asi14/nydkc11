Caring: Our Way of Life

What is Key Club?

Key Club is an international, student-led organization providing its members with opportunities to perform service, build character and develop leadership.

Our Core Values

Leadership

Key Club prepares the next generation of leaders right from high school. Our hiearchy gives members a unique experience, both on the local and international level.

Character Building

Key Club also teaches members about the virtue of responsibility, both in the contexts of being a member, and being a leader. All members are expected to go above and beyond in Key Club.

Caring

At Key Club, members of all origins are considered equal. We want everyone to feel that they are actively contributing back to their community.

Inclusiveness

Everyone is welcome to join Key Club. We actively work to make sure that everyone is included and grow together as a family.

Our Pledge

I pledge, on my honor, to uphold the Objects of Key Club International; to build my home, school and community; to serve my nation and God; and combat all forces which tend to undermine these institutions.
